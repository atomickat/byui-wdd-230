Repetition

Dominos Pizza

https://www.dominos.com/

Dominos’ website uses repetition in a threefold approach. At first sight, the bold prices pop with white foreground outlined in thick red, then a medium side red “order now” button with white lettering, and finally a tiny white title flag that introduces the product.

Visual Hierarchy

Little Caesars

https://littlecaesars.com

Little Caesars has visual hierarchy down. This website uses a deep orange background, their signature color, to draw the eye first to the large black bold price, then to the white title, and finally to the mouthwatering food picture.

Proximity

Pizza Hut

https://www.pizzahut.com/

Pizza Hut uses proximity to section content, such as grouping together featured items versus popular pizza selections.
